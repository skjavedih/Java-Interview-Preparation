
Kafka : 

Use Kafka when you want differnt parts of your systems to talk in real-time and share information smoothly.

- Think of Kafka for fast, ongoing updated
- Module like RestTemplet, web client etc for straightforward, point-to-point talks.

Let's take a real-life scenario where Kafka is in action.
Imagine you order the kitchen about your order instantly.

Each step(order received, cooking, ready for delivery) is like a message in Kafka, ensuring real-time updates.

This way, everyone involved is on the same page without constant manual checks, making the preocess smooth and efficient.
